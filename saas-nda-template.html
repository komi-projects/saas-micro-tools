Mutual Non-Disclosure Agreement

This NDA is between {{Party A}} and {{Party B}}, collectively the "Parties."

Key Clauses

Clause 1
Definition of Confidential Information
"Confidential Information" means all non-public, proprietary, or confidential information disclosed by either Party, including but not limited to: technical data, know-how, research, product plans, software code, business strategies, customer lists, and financial information.
Clause 2
Obligations of Receiving Party
Receiving Party agrees to: (a) hold all Confidential Information in strict confidence, (b) not disclose to any third parties except with prior written consent, (c) not use for any purpose other than evaluating business relationship.
Clause 3
Exclusions
Confidential Information does not include: (a) publicly available information, (b) already known by Receiving Party, (c) independently developed without use of disclosed information, (d) rightfully obtained from third parties.
Clause 4
Term
This NDA shall remain in effect for 3 years from the date of execution. Confidential Information disclosed during the term shall remain protected for 5 years from disclosure.
Clause 5
Return of Information
Upon written request, Receiving Party shall promptly return or destroy all Confidential Information and certify destruction in writing.
Clause 6
Remedies
Both Parties acknowledge that breach may cause irreparable harm. Non-breaching Party is entitled to seek injunctive relief in addition to other remedies.
Clause 7
Governing Law
This NDA is governed by the laws of {{State/Jurisdiction}}.

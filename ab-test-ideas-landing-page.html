🧪 Landing Page A/B Test Ideas

50+ experiments to boost your conversion rate.

Headline Tests

  • Benefit-focused vs Feature-focused headline
  • Question vs Statement headline
  • Short (5 words) vs Long (15 words) headline
  • Negative framing vs Positive framing
  • "You" vs "We" language

CTA Tests

  • Button color: Blue vs Green vs Orange
  • Button text: "Sign Up" vs "Start Free Trial" vs "Get Started"
  • One CTA vs Multiple CTAs
  • Sticky header CTA vs No sticky CTA
  • CTA above the fold vs Below the fold

Social Proof Tests

  • Testimonials with photos vs Without photos
  • Company logos vs Individual faces
  • Number of testimonials: 3 vs 6 vs 10
  • Video testimonials vs Text testimonials
  • Specific numbers ("1,000+ users") vs Vague ("Trusted by many")

Pricing Tests

  • Monthly vs Annual default toggle
  • 3 tiers vs 2 tiers vs 4 tiers
  • "$19" vs "$19/month" vs "$19/mo"
  • "Most Popular" badge vs No badge
  • Price anchoring: High-to-low vs Low-to-high

Page Structure Tests

  • Long page vs Short page
  • Video hero vs Static hero
  • FAQ section vs No FAQ
  • Live chat widget vs No chat
  • Exit-intent popup vs No popup

Content Funnel

SaaS content should map to the buyer's journey. Different content for different stages:

🎯 Top of Funnel (Awareness)
Educational content. Blog posts, guides, infographics, podcasts. Goal: attract and educate. No selling.
🎯 Middle of Funnel (Consideration)
Solution-oriented content. Comparison guides, case studies, webinars, ROI calculators. Goal: show you're the best solution.
🎯 Bottom of Funnel (Decision)
Product-focused content. Demo videos, free trials, pricing pages, implementation guides. Goal: convert to customer.

High-Impact Content Types for SaaS

1. ROI Calculators — Interactive tools that show value. "Calculate your savings with [Product]." Converts at 3-5x the rate of blog posts.

2. Comparison Pages — "[Product] vs [Competitor]." Buyers search for these before deciding. Be honest — acknowledge competitor strengths, then show where you win.

3. Implementation Guides — "How to migrate from [Legacy] to [Product]." Reduces fear of switching. Shows you understand the transition.

4. Customer Stories — Not testimonials — stories. "How [Customer] reduced churn 40% in 90 days." Specific, quantified, relatable.

5. Industry Reports — Original research. "State of [Industry] 2024." Positions you as a thought leader. High shareability.

Content Distribution

Creating content is 20% of the work. Distribution is 80%.

Owned channels: Email newsletter, blog, social media, product announcements
Earned channels: Guest posts, podcasts, PR, word-of-mouth, reviews
Paid channels: LinkedIn ads, retargeting, sponsored content, influencer partnerships

Rule: Every piece of content should be distributed across 5+ channels. A blog post becomes a Twitter thread, a LinkedIn post, an email snippet, a newsletter feature, and a PDF download.

Content Metrics

• Traffic: Unique visitors, page views. Top-of-funnel health.
• Engagement: Time on page, scroll depth, social shares. Content quality indicator.
• Leads: Email captures, trial signups, demo requests. Content ROI.
• Conversion: % of content visitors who become customers. Ultimate metric.
• CAC from content: Cost per lead from organic vs paid. Content should have 5-10x lower CAC than paid ads.

Content Calendar Framework

Weekly Rhythm
Monday: Publish blog post + distribute across channels
Tuesday: Email newsletter with top content
Wednesday: Social media content (threads, quotes, tips)
Thursday: Video or webinar promotion
Friday: Community engagement, Reddit/LinkedIn discussions

Monthly: 1 deep-dive guide, 1 case study, 1 comparison page
Quarterly: 1 industry report, 1 ROI calculator update, 1 webinar series

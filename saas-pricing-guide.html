💰 SaaS Pricing Guide

How to price your SaaS product for maximum revenue and conversion.

1. Cost-Plus Pricing

Calculate your costs and add a margin. Good for B2B tools with clear cost structures.

Tip: Add 30-50% margin on top of your per-customer costs (infrastructure, support, payment processing).

2. Value-Based Pricing

Price based on the value you create for customers. Best for tools with clear ROI.

Tip: If your tool saves $500/month, charge $49-99/month (10-20% of value created).

3. Competitor-Based Pricing

Match or undercut competitors. Good for entering established markets.

Tip: Price 20% below the market leader to attract price-sensitive customers, then raise prices after proving value.

4. Tiered Pricing

Offer 3 tiers: Basic, Pro, Enterprise. Captures different customer segments.

Tip: The middle tier should be your target. Price it at 2.5x the basic tier. Enterprise at 10x.

5. Freemium

Free tier with limited features. Converts 2-5% to paid.

Tip: Limit by usage (e.g., 100 requests/month) not by features. Users hit the limit and upgrade.

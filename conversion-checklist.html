🎯 Landing Page Conversion Checklist

Every element that turns visitors into signups.

Above the Fold

  • Headline states clear value proposition
  • Subheadline supports the headline
  • Primary CTA is visible without scrolling
  • CTA button color contrasts with background
  • Social proof above the fold (logos, count)
  • No navigation distractions (hide secondary links)

Trust & Social Proof

  • Customer testimonials with photos
  • "As seen on" or "Trusted by" logos
  • Specific numbers ("1,000+ teams use us")
  • Security badges (SSL, SOC2, etc.)
  • Founder photo and story
  • Money-back guarantee or free trial

Friction Reduction

  • No credit card required for trial
  • One-click signup (Google, GitHub OAuth)
  • Clear pricing (no hidden fees)
  • FAQ addresses top 3 objections
  • Live chat or chatbot for questions
  • Mobile-responsive design

Urgency & Scarcity

  • Limited-time offer (if applicable)
  • Countdown timer for launch pricing
  • "Only X spots left" (if true)
  • Recent activity notifications ("John just signed up")

The Challenge

Acme Corp was losing 8% of their MRR every month to failed payments. Their team was manually chasing customers, but 60% of churn was involuntary — customers who wanted to stay but had expired cards or insufficient funds.

The Solution

They implemented SimpleRecover's automated dunning system. Within 48 hours, smart retries were running, expired card updater was active, and personalized recovery emails were sending automatically.

"SimpleRecover paid for itself in the first week. We recovered $12,000 in failed payments and our churn rate dropped from 8% to 5.6%."
— Sarah Chen, VP of Revenue at Acme Corp

The Results

• 30% reduction in involuntary churn
• $12,000 recovered in month 1
• 5x ROI on the $49/month subscription
• Team saved 15 hours/week on manual follow-ups

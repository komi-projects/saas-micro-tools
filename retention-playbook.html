🤝 Customer Retention Playbook

Strategies and tactics to keep your SaaS customers longer.

1. Onboarding Excellence

  • • Welcome email within 5 minutes of signup
  • • Guided product tour (not optional)
  • • Empty state design that teaches
  • • Progress indicator ("You're 30% set up!")
  • • Celebrate first value moment (confetti, email)

2. Activation Triggers

  • • Identify "aha moment" — the action that predicts retention
  • • Push users toward it with in-app messages
  • • Email users who haven't hit it within 3 days
  • • Offer 1:1 onboarding for high-value accounts

3. Engagement Loops

  • • Weekly digest emails showing their activity
  • • In-app notifications for relevant features
  • • Gamification (badges, streaks, leaderboards)
  • • New feature announcements with "Try it now" CTAs

4. Churn Prevention

  • • Health score tracking (login frequency, feature usage)
  • • Automated win-back campaigns for dormant users
  • • Exit surveys with save offers (pause, discount, downgrade)
  • • Customer success check-ins at 30, 90, 180 days

5. Expansion Revenue

  • • Usage-based upgrade triggers ("You're at 90% of your plan")
  • • Annual discount offers ("Save 2 months by switching")
  • • Add-on features for power users
  • • Team/enterprise upsell path

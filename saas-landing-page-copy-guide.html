✍️ Landing Page Copy Guide

Write headlines, CTAs, and body copy that converts.

1. Headline Formula

[Outcome] + [Timeframe] + [Objection Handler]
Example
"Recover Failed Payments Automatically — Reduce Churn 30% in 30 Days"

2. CTA Formula

[Action Verb] + [Value] + [Urgency]
Example
"Start Recovering Revenue — Free 14-Day Trial"

3. Body Copy Structure

PAS Framework: Problem → Agitate → Solve

Problem
"40% of SaaS churn is involuntary — failed credit cards, expired payment methods."
Agitate
"You're losing customers who WANT to stay. Every failed payment is revenue walking out the door."
Solve
"SimpleRecover automatically retries failed payments, updates expired cards, and sends recovery emails — recovering 30% of lost revenue."

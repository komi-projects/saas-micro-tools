🎤 Customer Interview Script

The exact questions to ask customers to validate your SaaS idea.

Phase 1: Problem Validation (5 min)

1. "Tell me about the last time you [experienced the problem]."

2. "How did you solve it?"

3. "What was the most frustrating part?"

4. "How much time/money did that cost you?"

5. "How often does this happen?"

Phase 2: Solution Fit (5 min)

6. "If I could [solve the problem] automatically, would that be valuable?"

7. "What would that need to do exactly?"

8. "Who else on your team cares about this?"

9. "What would make you switch from your current solution?"

10. "What would make you NOT use this?"

Phase 3: Pricing Signal (3 min)

11. "Have you paid for a tool to solve this before?"

12. "What did you pay?"

13. "If this saved you [X hours/month], what would it be worth?"

14. "Would you be willing to pay $[price]/month for this?"

Red Flags & Green Flags

🚩 Red Flags (Pivot)
  • • "It's annoying but not urgent"
  • • "I haven't looked for a solution"
  • • "I'm not sure I'd pay for this"
  • • "I could build this myself"
✅ Green Flags (Build)
  • • "This happens every week"
  • • "I already pay for something worse"
  • • "I would have bought this yesterday"
  • • "Can I sign up now?"

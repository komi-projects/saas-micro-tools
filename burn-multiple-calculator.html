The golden metric for SaaS efficiency. Burn Multiple = Net Burn / Net New ARR.

What This Means

  • < 1.0x: Exceptional — You're generating more ARR than you're burning. The gold standard.
  • 1.0x – 1.5x: Great — Efficient growth. Most top-tier SaaS companies fall here.
  • 1.5x – 2.0x: Good — Reasonable burn for early-stage. Watch it closely.
  • 2.0x – 3.0x: Concerning — Burning significantly more than ARR growth. Consider cost cuts.
  • > 3.0x: Critical — Very inefficient. Investors will raise eyebrows. Fix this fast.

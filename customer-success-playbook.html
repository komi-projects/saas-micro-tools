# Customer Success Playbook for SaaS ## Overview This playbook covers everything from onboarding new customers to turning them into advocates. Built for bootstrapped SaaS founders who can't afford a dedicated CS team yet. ## 1. Onboarding (Days 0-7) ### The "Aha Moment" Every product has one action that predicts retention. For SimpleRecover, it's: - Customer connects their Stripe account - First failed payment is automatically retried - Customer sees the recovered revenue in their dashboard **Your job:** Get every user to this moment within 48 hours. ### Day 0: Welcome Sequence - **Email 1 (0 min):** "Welcome! Here's how to get started in 5 minutes" - **Email 2 (4 hours):** "Have you connected your payment processor yet?" - **Email 3 (24 hours):** "3 founders share how they recovered $X in their first week" ### Day 1-3: Guided Path - In-app checklist showing 3 setup steps - Progress bar ("You're 33% set up!") - Tooltip on every new feature they encounter - Celebrate completion with confetti or success message ### Day 7: First Win - Email: "You recovered $X this week!" - Show them the value they've already gotten - Ask for a testimonial if they're happy ## 2. Engagement (Week 2-4) ### Weekly Digest Send every user a Monday morning email with: - How much they recovered last week - How many customers they saved - A tip on using a feature they haven't tried ### Feature Discovery - Track which features each user has tried - Email them about features they haven't discovered - Use in-app messages: "Did you know you can [feature]?" ### Office Hours - Weekly 30-min open Zoom call - Users can drop in with questions - Record and share for those who can't make it ## 3. Retention (Month 2-6) ### Health Score Tracking Assign a score (0-100) based on: - Login frequency (last 7 days) - Feature usage (core features used) - Support tickets (more = lower score) - NPS responses **Action thresholds:** - 80-100: Green — offer referral program - 60-79: Yellow — send engagement content - 40-59: Orange — proactive outreach - 0-39: Red — immediate intervention ### Win-Back Campaigns For users who haven't logged in for 14 days: - Day 14: "We miss you — here's what you missed" - Day 21: "Quick question: what would bring you back?" - Day 30: "Your account will be paused in 7 days" - Day 37: Final offer — discount, downgrade, or pause ## 4. Expansion (Month 6+) ### Usage-Based Triggers When a user hits 80% of their plan limit: - In-app banner: "You're at 80% of your plan" - Email: "You're growing! Time to upgrade?" - Show them the ROI of upgrading ### Annual Push After 3 months of consistent usage: - Email: "Save 2 months by switching to annual" - Calculate their exact savings - Offer a bonus month for switching ### Team Plan Upsell When a user mentions team/collaboration: - Show team plan features - Per-seat pricing that scales with their team - Free trial of team features for 14 days ## 5. Advocacy (Year 1+) ### Referral Program - Give $50 account credit for every referral - Referred user gets first month free - Track referrals in a simple dashboard - Send monthly: "You've earned $X in referral credits" ### Case Studies For your top 10 customers: - Offer to write a case study about their results - Share on your blog, Product Hunt, social - Give them a free month for participating ### Community Building - Private Slack/Discord for paying customers - Monthly founder roundtable - Beta access to new features - Direct access to you (the founder) ## Templates ### Onboarding Email (Day 0) ``` Subject: Welcome to [Product] — Let's recover some revenue Hi [Name], Welcome! I'm [Your Name], founder of [Product]. Here's how to get started in 5 minutes: 1. Connect your Stripe account (1 min) 2. Set your retry preferences (2 min) 3. Watch the first recovery happen (2 min) [CTA: Connect Stripe →] Questions? Reply to this email — I read every one. [Your Name] ``` ### Win-Back Email (Day 14) ``` Subject: We miss you, [Name] Hi [Name], I noticed you haven't logged into [Product] for a couple weeks. That's totally okay — sometimes priorities shift. But I wanted to share: since you signed up, we've added [new feature] that [benefit]. Want to give it another shot? I can hop on a quick call to walk you through it. [CTA: Schedule 15-Min Call →] No pressure either way. [Your Name] ``` ### Expansion Email (80% Usage) ``` Subject: You're growing fast, [Name]! Hi [Name], I see you're at 80% of your [Plan] plan limit. That's amazing — it means [Product] is working for you. Here's what upgrading gets you: - [Feature 1] - [Feature 2] - [Feature 3] At your current growth rate, you'll hit the limit in about [X] days. [CTA: Upgrade Now →] Questions? Reply anytime. [Your Name] ``` ## Metrics to Track - **Activation Rate:** % of signups who hit "aha moment" - **Day 7 Retention:** % of signups active at day 7 - **Monthly Churn:** % of customers who cancel - **Net Revenue Retention:** MRR from existing customers (including expansion) - **NPS Score:** Customer satisfaction (target: 40+) - **Support Ticket Volume:** Per customer per month (target: <0.5) - **Time-to-Value:** Hours from signup to first success ## Tools You Need - **Email:** ConvertKit, Mailgun, or Postmark - **In-app messages:** Intercom, Crisp, or自建 - **Analytics:** Mixpanel, Amplitude, or Plausible - **Health scoring:** Spreadsheet + API calls - **Referral tracking:** Simple table or Rewardful ## Remember - Every email should feel like it's from a human, not a company - Over-communicate during onboarding, then find the right cadence - Your best customers will tell you what they need — just ask - One personal email beats 10 automated ones - Celebrate wins, commiserate losses, be real --- *Built by a bootstrapped founder, for bootstrapped founders.*

✍️ SaaS Copywriting Formulas

Proven frameworks for headlines, CTAs, and landing page copy.

AIDA

Attention — Grab their eye

Interest — Build curiosity

Desire — Make them want it

Action — Tell them what to do

Example: "Stop losing customers (A). See why 10K founders use our dunning tool (I). Recover 60% of failed payments automatically (D). Start your free trial (A)."

PAS

Problem — Identify the pain

Agitate — Make it hurt

Solution — Offer the fix

Example: "Failed payments cost you $500/mo (P). That's $6K/year gone forever (A). SimpleRecover recovers 60% automatically (S)."

FAB

Feature — What it is

Advantage — What it does better

Benefit — What they get

Example: "Smart retry logic (F). Recovers more payments than basic retries (A). You keep customers you would have lost (B)."

4U's

Urgent — Time-sensitive

Unique — Different from others

Ultra-specific — Exact numbers

Useful — Solves a problem

Example: "Launch week only: Save 50% on the only dunning tool with flat pricing (Urgent, Unique, Ultra-specific, Useful)."

Before-After-Bridge

Before — The painful present

After — The ideal future

Bridge — How to get there

Example: "Before: You manually chase failed payments (Before). After: Payments recover automatically while you sleep (After). Bridge: SimpleRecover connects in 5 minutes (Bridge)."

SOFT

Sympathize — "I get it"

Offer solution — Here's the fix

Features — What you get

Time limit — Act now

Example: "We know chasing payments is soul-crushing (S). SimpleRecover automates it (O). Smart retries, branded pages, email sequences (F). Launch week: 50% off (T)."

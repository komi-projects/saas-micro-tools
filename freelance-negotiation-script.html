💬 Freelance Rate Negotiation Script

Scripts for handling "that's too expensive" and other common objections.

Objection: "That's too expensive"

Your response:

"I understand budget is important. Let me ask — what's the cost of NOT solving this? If [problem] continues for [timeframe], that's [cost calculation]. My fee is $[X] to fix it permanently."

Alternative:

"I can work within your budget. Here's what we can do for $[their budget] vs $[your rate]: [scope adjustment]. Which makes more sense for your goals?"

Objection: "I found someone cheaper"

Your response:

"You absolutely can find cheaper options. The question is: will they deliver the result you need in [timeframe]? I've seen [number] projects where the cheap option cost more in rework. My rate includes [specific value] that cheaper options don't."

Objection: "Can you do it for [lower amount]?"

Your response:

"I can do $[lower amount] if we [reduce scope]. Here's what would be included: [adjusted scope]. Does that still solve your problem?"

Objection: "We don't have budget for this"

Your response:

"Totally understand. When DO you expect to have budget for this? I can hold this rate for [timeframe] and check back then. In the meantime, here's a [free resource] that might help."

Objection: "Can you start now and we'll figure out payment later?"

Your response:

"I need the upfront deposit to begin work — that's how I protect time for committed clients. Once I receive the deposit, I'll start within 24 hours. Does that timeline work for you?"

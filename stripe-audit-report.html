Frequently Asked

What data do you access? +
Read-only access to your Stripe account. We analyze payment failures, subscription status, and invoice data. We cannot create charges, refunds, or modify anything.
How long does it take? +
Basic audits deliver within 24 hours. Pro audits within 48 hours. Enterprise includes a scheduled consultation call.
Is my data secure? +
All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We use Stripe's official OAuth flow — no API keys stored on our servers. Reports are generated locally and emailed directly to you.

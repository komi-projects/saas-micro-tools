Scoring Model (Weighted)

Product Usage 30%
DAU/MAU ratio, feature adoption, core workflow completion. High usage = stickier customer.
Engagement 25%
Login frequency, support tickets (not too many, not zero), NPS responses, webinar attendance.
Financial 20%
Payment history, expansion revenue, plan upgrades, time as customer.
Relationship 15%
CSM touch frequency, executive sponsorship, reference willingness, community participation.
Sentiment 10%
NPS score, support ticket sentiment, survey responses, social mentions.

Automated Actions

🟢 Healthy (80-100)
• Flag for expansion conversation
• Invite to case study or testimonial
• Offer referral program
• Reduce CSM touch frequency
🟡 At Risk (60-79)
• Schedule CSM check-in within 7 days
• Send usage tips and best practices
• Identify blockers or missing features
• Escalate to product if feature gaps
🔴 Unhealthy (0-59)
• Immediate CSM escalation
• Executive sponsor call
• Retention offer (discount, extended trial)
• Root cause analysis — product, support, or fit?

Implementation Tips

1. Start simple. Use 3-4 metrics you already track. Don't over-engineer.

2. Weight by your business. If you're usage-based, product usage matters more. If enterprise, relationship matters more.

3. Review monthly. Health scores drift. What was healthy in January might be at risk in March.

4. Connect to revenue. Track correlation: customers who dropped from green to red — what % actually churned?

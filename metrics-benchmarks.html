📊 SaaS Metrics Benchmarks

What does "good" look like? Compare your metrics to industry averages.

💸 Churn Rate

Excellent < 2%/month
Good 2-5%/month
Average 5-7%/month
Poor > 7%/month

B2B SaaS typically has lower churn (2-5%) than B2C (5-10%).

📈 LTV:CAC Ratio

Excellent > 5:1
Good 3-5:1
Caution 1-3:1
Danger < 1:1

If your LTV:CAC is < 3:1, you may be spending too much to acquire customers.

⏱️ CAC Payback Period

Excellent < 6 months
Good 6-12 months
Caution 12-18 months
Danger > 18 months

Longer payback = more capital needed. Most VCs want < 12 months.

💰 ARPU (Average Revenue Per User)

High > $100/mo
Average $50-100/mo
Low $20-50/mo
Very Low < $20/mo

Low ARPU needs high volume. High ARPU can support sales-led growth.

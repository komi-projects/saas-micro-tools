🚀 User Onboarding Checklist

Everything to nail in a user's first 14 days.

Day 0: Signup

  • Welcome email sent within 5 minutes
  • No friction (no credit card, minimal fields)
  • Clear value proposition on first screen

Day 1: First Value

  • Guided product tour (not optional)
  • User completes core action ("aha moment")
  • Celebrate with confetti or success message

Day 3: Habit Formation

  • Email with tips based on their usage
  • Invite team / import data (if applicable)
  • In-app checklist showing progress

Day 7: Engagement

  • Weekly digest email with their stats
  • Showcase a feature they haven't tried
  • Offer a 1:1 call for high-value accounts

Day 14: Conversion

  • Upgrade prompt (if on free/trial)
  • Survey: "What would make you upgrade?"
  • Case study showing similar user's results

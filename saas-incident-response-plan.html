🚨 Incident Response Plan

Handle outages, breaches, and data incidents with a proven framework.

Severity Levels

Critical High Medium Low

Critical: Service completely down, data breach, security incident
High: Major feature degraded, significant customer impact
Medium: Partial degradation, workaround available
Low: Minor issue, minimal customer impact

Response Phases

1

Detection & Alerting

PagerDuty alerts trigger. On-call engineer acknowledges within 5 minutes. Status page updated to "Investigating."

2

Assessment & Triage

Determine severity. Assemble response team. Identify affected customers and systems.

3

Containment

Stop the bleeding. Rollback deploys. Enable failover. Isolate affected systems.

4

Resolution

Fix root cause. Verify fix in staging. Deploy to production. Monitor for 30 minutes.

5

Communication

Update status page. Email affected customers. Post-mortem scheduled within 24 hours.

6

Post-Mortem

What happened? Why? How did we respond? What will we improve? Action items assigned with owners.

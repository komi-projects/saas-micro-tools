✍️ Landing Page Copy Swipe File

Proven headlines, CTAs, and subheadlines you can copy and adapt.

Headlines That Convert

"Stop [pain point]. Start [desired outcome]."
Example: "Stop losing customers. Start recovering revenue."
"The [adjective] way to [outcome] without [objection]"
Example: "The easiest way to recover failed payments without engineering."
"[Number] [target audience] use [product] to [outcome]"
Example: "1,000+ SaaS founders use SimpleRecover to reduce churn."
"[Outcome] in [timeframe], guaranteed"
Example: "Recover 60% of failed payments in 30 days."

CTAs That Click

"Start Free Trial →"
Best for: Products with clear value proposition
"See How It Works"
Best for: Complex products that need explanation
"Get [Outcome] Now"
Best for: Tools with immediate results
"Join [Number] [Audience]"
Best for: Social proof-driven products

Subheadlines That Support

"No [objection]. No [objection]. Just [benefit]."
Example: "No coding. No setup fees. Just recovered revenue."
"[Social proof] [timeframe]"
Example: "Trusted by 1,000+ teams. Set up in 5 minutes."

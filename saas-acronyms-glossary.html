📚 SaaS Acronyms Glossary

50 essential terms every SaaS founder should know.

MRR
Monthly Recurring Revenue
The predictable revenue your SaaS generates each month from subscriptions.
ARR
Annual Recurring Revenue
MRR × 12. The standard metric for SaaS valuation.
LTV
Lifetime Value
The total revenue you expect from a customer over their entire relationship.
CAC
Customer Acquisition Cost
Total cost to acquire one customer (marketing + sales + onboarding).
NRR
Net Revenue Retention
Revenue from existing customers including expansion, minus churn. >100% means growth without new sales.
Churn
Customer Churn Rate
Percentage of customers who cancel each month. 5% monthly = 46% annual.
ACV
Annual Contract Value
Average annual value of a customer contract.
ARPU
Average Revenue Per User
MRR divided by total number of customers.

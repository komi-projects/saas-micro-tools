Parties

This Advisory Agreement is between {{Company Name}} ("Company") and {{Advisor Name}} ("Advisor").

Services

Clause 1
Advisor Duties
Advisor agrees to provide strategic guidance, industry introductions, and domain expertise. Expected time commitment: 2-4 hours per month, including quarterly calls and ad-hoc email support.
Clause 2
Best Efforts
Advisor will use best efforts to provide advice and introductions. No specific outcomes are guaranteed. Advisor has no fiduciary duty beyond standard care.

Compensation

💰 Equity Grant
Advisor receives {{X}} stock options at fair market value, vesting over {{Y}} years with a {{Z}}-month cliff. Standard advisor grants range from 0.1% - 0.5% for part-time advisors, up to 1% for highly engaged advisors.

No cash compensation. This is an equity-only advisory relationship unless otherwise agreed in writing.

Term & Termination

Clause 3
Term
Initial term of 1 year, automatically renewing unless terminated by either party with 30 days written notice.
Clause 4
Termination
Either party may terminate at any time with 30 days notice. Vested options remain exercisable per the stock option agreement. Unvested options are forfeited.

Confidentiality & IP

Clause 5
Confidentiality
Advisor agrees to keep all company information confidential for 3 years after termination. Standard exceptions for publicly available information apply.
Clause 6
IP Assignment
Any ideas, feedback, or suggestions provided by Advisor are freely given without expectation of additional compensation or IP rights. Company may use them without restriction.

Mutual Expectations

Company agrees to:

• Provide regular updates (monthly investor updates)
• Prepare specific questions/agenda for calls
• Respect Advisor's time (no late-night pings unless truly urgent)
• Follow through on introductions and advice given

Advisor agrees to:

• Respond to emails within 48 hours
• Make 2-3 meaningful introductions per quarter
• Provide honest, direct feedback
• Not advise direct competitors during the term

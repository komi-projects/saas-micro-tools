SaaS SEO Strategy

SEO for SaaS is different from e-commerce or media. You're not trying to rank for "buy shoes online." You're trying to capture demand from people searching for solutions to problems your product solves.

The 3 pillars of SaaS SEO:

1. Problem-aware content: "How to reduce customer churn" — attracts people who have the problem you solve
2. Solution-aware content: "Best customer retention software" — attracts people actively evaluating solutions
3. Brand-aware content: "[YourProduct] vs [Competitor]" — attracts people already considering you

Keyword Categories

🔥 High-Intent (Conversion)
"[Category] software," "[Category] tool," "best [Category] for [use case]." Low volume, high conversion. These are your money keywords.
📈 Comparison
"[Product] vs [Competitor]," "[Product] alternatives," "best [Category] 2024." Buyers at decision stage. High conversion, moderate volume.
📚 Educational
"How to [solve problem]," "[Problem] best practices," "[Industry] guide." High volume, low conversion. Builds authority and captures top-of-funnel.
🎯 Branded
"[YourProduct] pricing," "[YourProduct] reviews," "[YourProduct] login." Protect your brand real estate. Easy wins.

On-Page SEO for SaaS

Title tags: Include primary keyword + value prop. "Customer Retention Software | Reduce Churn by 40%"

Meta descriptions: CTA-focused, include keyword. "Reduce customer churn with [Product]. Free trial. See why 1000+ SaaS companies trust us."

URL structure: /category/keyword. /blog/customer-retention-strategies. /compare/best-churn-software.

Internal linking: Link from blog posts to product pages. Link from comparison pages to case studies. Create content clusters around your core keywords.

Page speed: SaaS buyers are impatient. < 3 seconds load time. Use lazy loading, compress images, minimize JS.

Technical SEO Checklist

✅ Mobile responsive (60%+ of B2B searches are mobile)
✅ HTTPS + SSL certificate
✅ XML sitemap submitted to Google Search Console
✅ Robots.txt configured correctly
✅ Canonical tags on duplicate pages (e.g., /blog?page=2)
✅ Schema markup (SoftwareApplication, Organization, FAQ)
✅ Core Web Vitals passing (LCP < 2.5s, FID < 100ms, CLS < 0.1)
✅ No broken links (check monthly)
✅ Redirects for old URLs (301, not 302)

Link Building for SaaS

1. Guest posting. Write for industry publications. Link back to your content (not homepage).

2. Original research. Publish data others will cite. "State of SaaS Churn 2024." Natural link magnet.

3. Free tools. Build calculators, graders, analyzers. Other sites link to useful tools.

4. Podcast appearances. Every podcast episode = a backlink from their show notes.

5. Partnership content. Co-create content with complementary SaaS companies. Cross-promote and cross-link.

SEO Metrics

• Organic traffic: Month-over-month growth. Target: 10-20% monthly.
• Keyword rankings: Positions for target keywords. Track top 20.
• Organic conversions: Trial signups, demo requests from organic. The metric that matters.
• Domain authority: Moz/ahrefs score. Benchmark against competitors.
• Indexed pages: How many pages Google has indexed. Should grow monthly.

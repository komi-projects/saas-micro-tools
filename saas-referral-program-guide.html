🤝 Referral Programs

Turn customers into your best sales channel.

Why Referrals Matter

Referral customers have 16-25% lower CAC, 37% higher retention, and 4x higher LTV than non-referred customers. They trust the referrer, onboard faster, and expand more readily.

For SaaS, referrals are the most efficient growth channel — but only if designed correctly.

Incentive Structures

🎁 Single-Sided (Referrer Gets Reward)
Referrer gets $50 credit or 1 free month. Simple, easy to communicate. Works for products with network effects (e.g., Slack, Dropbox).
🎁🎁 Double-Sided (Both Win)
Referrer gets $50, new user gets $50 or 20% off. Most effective structure. Removes friction — the referrer isn't "selling," they're sharing a deal.
🏆 Tiered Rewards
1 referral = $25, 3 referrals = $100, 5 referrals = $250 + swag. Gamifies the experience. Works for power users and advocates.
💰 Cash vs. Credit
Cash rewards appeal to a broader audience. Account credit keeps money in your ecosystem. Test both. Cash often wins for B2B SaaS.

Program Mechanics

1. Make it easy to share. One-click sharing via email, Slack, LinkedIn, Twitter. Pre-write the message. "Hey [Name], I've been using [Product] and it's saved us 10 hours/week. Here's a discount: [link]"

2. Time it right. Ask for referrals at moments of delight: after a positive NPS score, after onboarding completion, after a support resolution, after a feature launch.

3. Track everything. Unique referral links, UTM codes, attribution. Know which customers drive the most referrals and double down on them.

4. Pay quickly. Don't make referrers wait 90 days. Pay when the referred customer pays, or within 30 days. Delayed gratification kills motivation.

Referral Program Metrics

• Referral rate: % of customers who refer. Good: 10-15%. Great: 20%+.
• Referral-to-customer conversion: % of referrals who become customers. Target: 15-25%.
• Referred CAC: Cost per referred customer. Should be 50-70% lower than paid CAC.
• Viral coefficient (K): Average referrals per customer. K > 1 = exponential growth.
• Referral revenue %: % of new revenue from referrals. Target: 20-30%.

Referral Program Examples

Notion
Refer a friend, both get $10 credit. Simple, double-sided, instant gratification. Integrated into the product UI.
Dropbox
Refer a friend, get 500MB free storage. Gamified with a progress bar. Drove 3900% growth in 15 months.
Slack
$100 credit for every paying team referred. Targeted at power users and workspace admins.

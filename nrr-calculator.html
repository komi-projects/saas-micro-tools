NRR Benchmarks

  • > 120%: Exceptional — Best-in-class. Companies like Snowflake, Datadog operate here.
  • 110% – 120%: Great — Strong expansion, healthy business.
  • 100% – 110%: Good — Offsetting churn with expansion.
  • < 100%: Concerning — Losing more revenue than gaining from existing customers.

What This Means

  • LTV > 3x CAC: Healthy unit economics. You can invest more in acquisition.
  • LTV 1-3x CAC: Marginal. You're breaking even or barely profiting per customer.
  • LTV < CAC: Unsustainable. You're losing money on every customer.
  • Payback period < 12 months: Ideal. You recover acquisition costs within a year.

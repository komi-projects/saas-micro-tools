🤝 Referral Program Playbook

Design, launch, and optimize a referral program that drives growth.

1. Choose Your Incentive Structure

Dual-sided incentives work best: both the referrer and the new user get something.

Best Practice: Give the referrer 1 month free and the new user 20% off their first 3 months.

2. Make It Frictionless

The easier it is to refer, the more people will do it. One-click sharing > copy-paste codes.

Best Practice: Add referral buttons inside your app (not just in emails). In-app referrals convert 3x better.

3. Time It Right

Ask for referrals when users are happiest — after a success moment, not during onboarding.

Best Practice: Trigger the referral prompt after a user hits a milestone (e.g., "You just processed your 100th payment! Share with a friend and both get a reward").

4. Track and Optimize

Measure referral rate, conversion rate, and cost per acquisition. A healthy referral program contributes 10-30% of new signups.

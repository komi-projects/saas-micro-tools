🏠 Remote Work Policy

Define expectations, communication norms, and stipends for distributed teams.

Scope

This policy applies to all employees working remotely, whether full-time remote or hybrid. It covers work hours, communication expectations, equipment, and security requirements.

Work Hours

Core Hours
All employees must be available during core hours: 10am - 3pm {{timezone}}. Outside core hours, individuals set their own schedule.
Response Time
Slack: 4 hours during work hours. Email: 24 hours. PagerDuty (on-call): 15 minutes.
Time Off
Unlimited PTO with 2-week notice for vacations > 3 days. Same-day sick leave allowed without notice.

Communication Norms

Slack
Default for quick questions. Use threads. Status emoji: 🟢 working, 🌴 away, 🔴 do-not-disturb.
Zoom
Camera on for 1:1s and team meetings. Optional for all-hands > 10 people. Record and share for async consumption.
Async First
Write it down before scheduling a meeting. Documentation lives in Notion. Decisions made in Linear/GitHub, not Slack.

Equipment & Stipends

Home Office Stipend
$1,000 one-time stipend for desk, chair, monitor, and accessories. Additional $200/year for upgrades.
Laptop
Company-provided MacBook Pro (14" or 16"). Refresh every 3 years or on request.
Internet & Phone
$100/month reimbursement for internet and mobile phone.

Security Requirements

🔒 VPN required for all internal services. 2FA mandatory on all accounts. No company data on personal devices.

• Company laptop must be encrypted (FileVault)
• Password manager (1Password) required
• Annual security training
• Screen lock after 5 minutes of inactivity

Meetings

• No-meeting Wednesdays
• Default meeting length: 25 or 50 minutes
• Agenda required for all meetings > 2 people
• Record if anyone is in a different timezone (+/- 3 hours)

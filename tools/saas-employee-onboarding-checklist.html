👋 Employee Onboarding

Get new hires productive in week 1 with a 30-day plan.

📅 Day 1: First Impressions

Welcome email + Slack invite

Send before they arrive. Include first-day schedule.HR

Laptop + accounts setup

MacBook, email, GitHub, Slack, Notion, 1Password.IT

Office tour / remote setup guide

Introduce team. Show where things are (or how to find them remotely).Manager

Team lunch / virtual coffee

Casual 1:1s with 3-4 key teammates.Manager

📅 Week 1: Foundation

Product deep-dive

Walk through the app. Create a test account. Use every feature.Product

Codebase orientation

Architecture overview. Deploy a small change to staging.Engineering

First ticket / task

Small, well-defined, and achievable. Ship by Friday.Manager

📅 Week 2-3: Integration

Shadow customer calls

Listen to 3 support calls or sales demos.CS/Sales

Own a feature area

Assigned ownership of a small feature or component.Manager

Write your first documentation

Update the onboarding doc with something you learned.Self

📅 Day 30: Check-in

30-day 1:1 with manager

What's working? What's confusing? What do you need?Manager

Peer feedback session

Informal feedback from 2-3 teammates.Peers

Set 90-day goals

Define what success looks like by month 3.Manager

Parties

This Master Service Agreement ("MSA") is between {{Provider Name}} ("Provider") and {{Customer Name}} ("Customer"), collectively referred to as the "Parties."

Key Articles

Article 1
Services
Provider agrees to provide Customer with access to the SaaS platform described in applicable Order Forms.
Article 2
Term & Renewal
Initial term of 12 months, automatically renewing for successive 12-month periods unless terminated with 30 days notice.
Article 3
Fees & Payment
Annual billing in advance. Net 30 payment terms. Late fees: 1.5% per month on overdue amounts.
Article 4
Intellectual Property
Provider retains all IP rights. Customer retains all data. Provider receives limited license to process Customer data for service delivery.
Article 5
Confidentiality
Both parties agree to protect confidential information for 3 years after termination.
Article 6
Liability
Provider's liability is capped at fees paid in the 12 months preceding the claim. Neither party liable for indirect damages.
Article 7
Indemnification
Provider indemnifies for IP infringement. Customer indemnifies for misuse and data content.
Article 8
Termination
Either party may terminate for breach with 30 days cure period. Customer may terminate for convenience with 30 days notice.
Article 9
Data Return & Deletion
Upon termination, Provider will return or delete Customer data within 30 days per Customer's written instruction.
Article 10
Governing Law
This MSA is governed by the laws of {{State/Jurisdiction}}. Disputes resolved through binding arbitration.

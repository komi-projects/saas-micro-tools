SaaS Churn Recovery Playbook

📋 Your 90-Day Recovery Plan

Week 1-2: Assessment & Quick Wins

Identify churn patterns, segment at-risk customers, implement immediate fixes.

  • Audit last 90 days of churn reasons
  • Segment customers by risk score
  • Fix top 3 payment failure causes
  • Deploy pre-dunning emails

Week 3-6: Core Recovery

Launch full dunning sequences, implement smart retries, add in-app paywalls.

  • Deploy 5-email dunning sequence
  • Configure Stripe Smart Retries
  • Add payment update banners in-app
  • Personal outreach for $500+ MRR accounts

Week 7-10: Optimization

A/B test email copy, optimize send times, reduce friction in payment flow.

  • A/B test dunning email subject lines
  • Optimize retry timing (1, 3, 5, 7, 14 days)
  • Test alternative payment methods
  • Implement usage-based billing for expansion

Week 11-12: Scale & Measure

Document wins, train team, set up ongoing monitoring, plan next quarter.

  • Calculate exact revenue recovered
  • Document playbook for team
  • Set up weekly churn monitoring dashboard
  • Plan Q2 retention initiatives

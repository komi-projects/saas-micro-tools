📁 Due Diligence Data Room

Prepare for M&A, fundraising, or audits with a complete document index.

Corporate Must Have

🏢 Corporate Documents
  • Certificate of Incorporation & Bylaws
  • Cap table (current and all historical)
  • Stock purchase agreements
  • Investor rights agreements
  • Voting agreements
  • Board minutes (all meetings)
  • Stock option plan & grant docs

Financials Must Have

💰 Financial Documents
  • Audited financials (last 3 years, if available)
  • Monthly financial statements (last 24 months)
  • Revenue recognition policy
  • Burn rate and runway analysis
  • Projections and financial model
  • Bank statements
  • Debt schedule

Legal Must Have

⚖️ Legal Documents
  • Material contracts (customers >$50K ARR)
  • MSAs and order forms
  • Employment agreements (all employees)
  • IP assignment agreements
  • Patents, trademarks, copyrights
  • Litigation summary (if any)
  • Privacy policy, ToS, DPA

Product & Tech Should Have

💻 Technical Documents
  • Architecture diagrams
  • Security audit reports (SOC 2, pentest)
  • API documentation
  • Code repository access (read-only)
  • Infrastructure and hosting details
  • Disaster recovery plan

Customers & Revenue Should Have

👥 Customer Data
  • Customer list with ARR/MRR
  • Churn analysis (cohort, logo, revenue)
  • Net Revenue Retention data
  • Customer concentration analysis
  • Pipeline and forecast

HR & Team Nice to Have

👤 Team Documents
  • Org chart
  • Headcount plan
  • Compensation bands
  • Key person insurance

🎯 Trial Optimization

Maximize trial-to-paid conversion.

Trial Length

The standard SaaS trial is 14 days, but the optimal length depends on your product complexity:

7 days
Simple products, clear value, low friction
14 days
Standard for most B2B SaaS
30 days
Complex products, enterprise, integrations

Key insight: Shorter trials create urgency. A 7-day trial with strong onboarding often converts better than a 30-day trial where users procrastinate.

Trial Conversion Benchmarks

15-25%
Opt-in trial (credit card required)
3-10%
Opt-out trial (no credit card)
30-50%
Sales-assisted trial

Opt-in vs opt-out: Opt-in trials (requiring credit card) have higher conversion but lower signup volume. Opt-out trials have 3-5x more signups but lower conversion. Choose based on your acquisition strategy.

Trial Optimization Tactics

1. Time-based limits. Show "3 days left" prominently. Urgency drives action. A countdown timer in the product increases conversion 15-30%.

2. Usage-based trials. Instead of 14 days, offer "100 actions" or "5 projects." Users who get value fast convert earlier. Those who need time aren't rushed.

3. Reverse trial. Give full features for 14 days, then downgrade to free tier (don't cut off access). Users experience what they're losing.

4. Trial extension. If user is engaged but hasn't converted, offer 7 more days. "You're making great progress — need more time?"

5. Paywall the right features. Trial users should experience premium features. Don't give away everything — save the "wow" features for paid.

Trial Engagement Sequence

Day 1-3: Activation
Focus: Get user to first value.
Emails: Welcome + quick-start guide.
In-app: Setup checklist, interactive tour.
Day 4-7: Engagement
Focus: Build habit.
Emails: Feature tips, success stories.
In-app: Usage stats, "you're in top 10% of users."
Day 8-12: Conversion
Focus: Push to paid.
Emails: Case studies, ROI calculator, limited-time offer.
In-app: "Upgrade to save your work" banner.
Day 13-14: Last Chance
Focus: Create urgency.
Emails: "Trial ends tomorrow," discount offer, calendar link for demo.
In-app: Modal with upgrade options.

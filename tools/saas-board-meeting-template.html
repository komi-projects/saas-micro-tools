📊 Board Meeting Template

Run effective board meetings with a proven agenda and metrics dashboard.

Pre-Meeting: Board Deck (24 hours before)

Send the deck 24 hours in advance. No surprises. The meeting is for discussion, not presentation.

Agenda (2 hours total)

0:00

CEO Update (15 min)

Wins, challenges, strategic shifts. No metrics — save for the dashboard.

0:15

Metrics Dashboard Review (20 min)

KPIs, trends, variance from plan. Board reads silently, asks questions.

0:35

Functional Deep-Dive (20 min)

Rotate: Product, Engineering, Sales, Marketing. One per meeting.

0:55

Strategic Discussion (30 min)

One big topic: pricing change, new market, major hire, competitive threat.

1:25

Decisions & Action Items (20 min)

What needs board approval? What are next steps? Who owns what?

1:45

Executive Session (15 min)

Board only. CEO feedback, succession planning, sensitive topics.

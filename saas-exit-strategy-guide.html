2. Preparation Checklist

• Clean financials (3 years of books)

• Document all IP and contracts

• Reduce founder dependency

• Build a strong management team

• Diversify customer base (no single customer >20%)

3. Deal Structure

• Cash at close: 60-80% of total

• Earnout: 20-40% based on performance

• Transition period: 6-12 months typical

• Non-compete: 2-3 years standard

🚀 Product Hunt Launch Strategy

The playbook for launching your SaaS on Product Hunt and going #1.

Pre-Launch (2 Weeks Before)

  • • Create a maker profile with photo, bio, and previous hunts
  • • Write your tagline (60 chars max) — test 5 variations
  • • Prepare 3 GIFs/screenshots showing the product in action
  • • Write the first comment (your maker story) — make it personal
  • • Line up 20+ hunters to upvote at launch
  • • Schedule launch for Tuesday 12:01 AM PST (best day)

Launch Day

  • • Post at 12:01 AM PST sharp
  • • Share in 5 relevant communities (Indie Hackers, Reddit, Slack groups)
  • • Reply to EVERY comment within 5 minutes
  • • Update the post with "We're #3! Help us get to #1 →"
  • • DM your network personally — don't mass-spam
  • • Keep the energy high for 8 hours (peak voting window)

Post-Launch

  • • Thank every commenter personally
  • • Add "Product Hunt #1" badge to your landing page
  • • Write a post-mortem on your blog
  • • Reach out to journalists who covered #1 products
  • • Convert PH traffic — special offer for PH visitors
  • • Track conversion: PH visitors → signups → paid

The Maker Story Template

"I built [Product] because [personal pain point]. For [X months], I [struggled with problem]. Existing solutions were [too expensive / too complex / missing feature].

So I spent [timeframe] building [Product]. The first version was [ugly / basic / MVP], but it [solved the core problem].

Today, [number] [target audience] use [Product] to [outcome]. We're [revenue / growth stat].

I'd love your feedback. What would make this indispensable for you?"
